On Sunday night’s episode of “The Real Housewives of Potomac,” some of the ladies wanted proof that Karen Huger actually lives in her home. As such, Gizelle Bryant, Robyn Dixon and Ashley Darby devised an elaborate scheme to discover the truth. And fans remained unconvinced about Huger’s true living situation.

Huger confronted her assistant Matt for his part in spreading a rumor that she lives in a furnished apartment rather than her 10,000 square foot home in Great Falls, Va. Then, Darby, Bryant and Dixon decide to disguise themselves as pizza delivery guys to drive by her home and figure things out. The ladies really just wanted to know if she’s lying or not.
