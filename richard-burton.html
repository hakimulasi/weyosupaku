Biography

Richard Burton was born in Pontrhydyfen, Wales on November 10, 1925. The Welsh actor is best known for his starring roles opposite Elizabeth Taylor, particularly 1963’s Cleopatra and 1966’s Who’s Afraid of Virginia Woolf? His other memorable films include Hamlet, The Robe, and Nineteen Eighty-Four. He starred opposite Clint Eastwood in the 1968 film Where Eagles Dare. He was an exceptional rugby and cricket player in his home country of Wales.
