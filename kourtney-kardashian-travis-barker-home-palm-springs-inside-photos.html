Travis Barker &amp; Kourtney Kardashian's $12million love nest is out of this world

Inside the couple's gorgeous new home

    Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker are not short of homes between them, but the couple added to their property portfolio last year when star purchased a gorgeous Palm Springs mansion.

Located in La Quinta, it has a manicured golf course, an outdoor swimming pool, a guest house and a courtyard complete with custom water features and a fire display.
