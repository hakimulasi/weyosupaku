A Chilling Assault

Disturbing Utterances

As the attack unfolded, the assailant uttered the phrase “au nom de Jésus Christ” (in the name of Jesus Christ) twice in English, as captured in a video and confirmed by a close source. Swiftly apprehended by the police, the suspect was wearing a Christian cross at the time of his arrest.

Unraveling the Assailant’s Background

A Syrian Refugee in Sweden

Elisabeth Borne clarified that the assailant was a Syrian refugee residing in Sweden, with no prior record in intelligence services. She also stated that the suspect had no known psychiatric history.
