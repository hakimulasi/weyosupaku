What is Ray Wilson's Net Worth?

Ray Wilson is a Scottish musician who has a net worth of $3 million. Ray Wilson was born in Dumfries, Scotland in September 1968. He is best known for being the vocalist for the bands Stiltskin and Genesis. Ray started off in a band called Guaranteed Pure in 1990. The band released an album called Swing Your Bag. He joined the band Stiltskin who also released one album and had a #1 hit the UK with "Inside". After Phil Collins left Genesis in 1995 Wilson became their lead singer until 1998.

The only Genesis album Wilson was featured on was 1997's Calling All Stations which was certified Gold in France, Germany, Poland, and the United Kingdom. The album peaked at #2 in the UK. The album did not do well and their American tour was cancelled due to low ticket sales so Ray was released from the group. He released the album Millionairhead with his solo project Cut in 1999. Wilson released four other solo studio albums. He later released new albums with Stiltskin in 2006 and 2011.
