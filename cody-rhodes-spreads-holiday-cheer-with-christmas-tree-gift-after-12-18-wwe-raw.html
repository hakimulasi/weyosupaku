Cody Rhodes Spreads Holiday Cheer With Christmas Tree Gift after 12/18 WWE RAW

Cody Rhodes is certainly one of the most beloved stars in WWE right now. Cody’s natural charisma and relatable attitude have made him a fan-favorite in WWE. He gained more favor from fans after RAW this week as he ended up gifting a whole Christmas tree to a fan.

This week’s episode of Monday Night RAW was action-packed from start to finish in the best way possible. It also saw Cody Rhodes and Shinsuke Nakamura’s feud enter a whole new level.

After all, The King of Strong Style wrote a whole new set of poems for The American Nightmare, which featured him calling Dusty Rhodes an inbred – shocking fans in attendance.

After Monday Night RAW concluded, Cody Rhodes decided to surprise a fan in attendance as he gifted a lucky fan a whole new Christmas tree. Rhodes noted that he had no idea how the fan was going to take it home either.

”Right there? Alright. Young buddy, this tree belongs to you. I have no clue how you’re gonna get it home, I hope you have an STV, I don’t even know how you’re gonna get it out of the ring. The tree is yours.”

Cody Rhodes even gifted a fan a table after Friday Night Smackdown last week. This isn’t even the first time Cody Rhodes gifted something this year, as he even gifted an army veteran table just before Survivor Series last month. It remains to be seen how Cody Rhodes and Shinsuke Nakamura’s feud will be booked in the coming weeks.
