How rich is Sammy Gravano: What is His Net Worth

Sammy Gravano is a wealthy Italian-American criminal with a fortune of $500 thousand. Gravano was only a teenager when the mafia spotted him.

He became recognized as the man who assisted the Federal Bureau of Investigation in bringing down John Gotti, the legendary family leader, as a former underboss of the New York City-based Gambino criminal family. He accomplished this by testifying before the government.

Who is Sammy Gravano?

Sammy Gravano was born Salvatore Gravano on March 12, 1945, in Brooklyn, New York, the youngest of three children to Sicilian parents Giorlando “Gerry” and Caterina “Kay” Gravano. Gravano struggled with dyslexia and was tormented as a child and in school. He joined the Rampers, a well-known street gang when he was 13.

He first went for a bunch of thieves who had stolen his bike, taking on many of them at once while Made guys watched from a cafe. He got his bike back, and one of the men commented on how he “fought like a bull,” earning him the iconic moniker. He worked as a mess hall cook in Fort Jackson, South Carolina, eventually rising to the rank of corporal. Gravano received an honorable discharge after two years of service.
