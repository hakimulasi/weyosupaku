Early Life

Trecina Evette “Tina” Campbell was born as Trecina Evette Atkins to Thomasina Andrea and Edward Aaron Atkins, Jr. She was born in California’s Inglewood on 1 May 1974.

She is the younger sister of Erica Campbell, who too is a singer.

Career

In 1998, Tina Campbell teamed up with her sister Erica, to form the musical duo ‘Mary Mary.’ In 2000, the duo released their debut album entitled ‘Thankful.’ It was released on Columbia Records and was a certified platinum album.

Mary Mary has also released the compilation album ‘Go Get It’ in 2012. Their holiday album ‘A Mary Mary Christmas’ became available from October 2006.

The duo has also released many singles including “Shackles (Praise You),” “I Sings,” “In the Morning,” “Dance, Dance, Dance,” “Heaven,” “Carol of the Bells,” “Get Up,” “God in Me,” and “Back to You.” Their guest singles include “Dance” and “Love Him As I Do.”

The sisters have also released many music videos. From 2007 to 2012, Tina Campbell of the gospel music competition show, ‘Sunday Best’ on BET. She was the judge, along with her sister and Donnie McClurkin. Additionally, she was also a part of the reality TV series called ‘Mary Mary’ on WE tv.

As a solo artist, Tina has released the album ‘It’s Personal’ in 2015. This was released on Entertainment One. Her second album, ‘It’s Still Personal,’ became available from September 2017.

Awards & Achievements

In 2018, Tina Campbell was nominated for the Grammy Awards for “Too Hard Not To.” With Mary Mary, she has received two American Music Awards out of five nominations. At the 2011 ASCAP Awards, the duo won the Golden Note Award. They are also the winner of three BET Awards, seven Dove Awards, and four Grammy Awards.
