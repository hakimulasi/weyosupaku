Miranda Lambert &quot;Doesn't Care&quot; Who Blake Shelton Dates After Their Divorce - Closer Weekly

Miranda Lambert is moving on from her divorce from Blake Shelton.

In the wake of Blake, 39, confirming he’s in a relationship with his The Voice costar Gwen Stefani, 46, those close to Miranda, 31, have revealed she “doesn’t care” who her ex-husband dates now that they’re no longer an item.

Blake and Gwen in September 2014.

“She had her suspicions but didn’t really know [their relationship] was a real thing until the announcement,” a source close to the country star told Us Weekly. “But she doesn’t care. She’s divorced and he can date who he wants.”

“She got divorced from him so he’s free to date whoever he wants. She’s fine,” the insider added. Blake and Miranda — who previously tied the knot in May 2011 — announced their divorce after four years of marriage in July.

Miranda accepting her 2015 CMA award.

Earlier this week, the former couple both attended the CMAs, where Miranda received the evening’s coveted Female Vocalist of the Year award. While accepting the honor, the “Little Red Wagon” singer said the win was a much-needed “bright spot” in her otherwise stressful year.

“Man, I really appreciate it,” she said of winning the award on Wednesday, Nov. 4. “I needed a bright spot this year, so thank you. I love country music fans.”
