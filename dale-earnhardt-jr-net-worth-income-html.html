Racing driver Ralph Dale Earnhardt Jr. was born on 10 October 1974 in Kannapolis, North Carolina, United States. He is also a radio broadcaster, actor in television commercials, and a voice actor. His father, Dale Earnhardt Sr., was a well-known NASCAR driver who was a seven-time Winston Cup Series champion as well as one of NASCAR’s 50 Greatest Drivers. Dale Jr. followed in his footsteps from 2000 until his retirement from full-time racing at the conclusion of the 2017 season.
