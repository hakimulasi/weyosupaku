About

Award-winning comedienne who became known for her role as phone operator Ernestine on 9 to 5. In 2004, she appeared in I Heart Huckabees and in 2015, she began starring as Frankie in the Netflix series Grace and Frankie.

Before Fame

She began performing as a stand-up Comedian in nightclubs after graduating from college.

Trivia

She won multiple awards including a 1972 Grammy Award in the Best Comedy Album category for This Is A Recording and four Emmy Awards.

Family Life

She began dating Jane Wagner in 1971 and they married in 2013.

Associated With
