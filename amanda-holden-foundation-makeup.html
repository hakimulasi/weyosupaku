We've tracked down Amanda Holden's foundation and it costs just 9

Get glowing skin like the Britain's Got Talent judge…

    Amanda Holden's beauty game is strong. Precise eyebrows, luscious long lashes and a perfect nude pout, she never puts a foot wrong when it comes to her makeup bag. We are forever wondering what foundation she uses to perfect her glowing skin – and now we know! The Britain's Got Talent star shared a picture on her Instagram account of her getting her makeup applied ahead of her shoot with QVCuk and makeup artist Charlotte Reid listed the products she used to get her skin looking tip top - and it's not as expensive as one may expect. The Invisiwear Liquid Foundation by EX1 Cosmetics, which was used on the star,costs just £9 at online store The Hut. The lightweight formula gives the skin a beautiful 'lit' finish. Formulated with true colour pigments, it works with your skin's natural tone to give a fabulous flawless base that stays expertly natural. No wonder Amanda is a fan!

    What's more, the mother-of-two isn't the only celebrity loving this foundation – a certain Ms Margot Robbie is obsessed too! The I, Tonya actress even wore it to the BAFTA’s in February. Makeup artist to the stars Pati Dubroff – who made up Margot's face that night - said of the formula: "The foundation is amazing for balancing the skin tone and accentuating a glowy finish." Well, if it gives us skin like this gorgeous pair – we know what we will be buying come payday.

    Invisiwear Liquid Foundation by EX1 Cosmetics, £9

    Amanda, 47, often mixes up her glam squad – she also uses Bryony Blake to preen her face as well as Karin Darnell. Both ladies share the products they use on their clients on Instagram – so it's easy to pick up expert makeup tricks.
