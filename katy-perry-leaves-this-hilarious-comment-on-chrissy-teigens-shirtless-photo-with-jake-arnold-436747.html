Katy Perry Leaves This Hilarious Comment On Chrissy Teigens Shirtless Photo With Jake Arnold

Chrissy Teigen is currently on bed rest as she is pregnant with her third child and stated she was spotting, and while she is taking it easy now, she recently posted a funny photo on her Instagram account. Everyone knows that Chrissy is married to EGOT winner John Legend, but she stood for a photo with Jake Arnold where the two gave the impression they could be a couple!

Jake Arnold is a well known interior decorator and both he and Chrissy shared similar photos with each other on their Instagram accounts. In one photo, Chrissy lost her top and posed in her bra with her pants unbuttoned revealing her growing baby bump. She stood in front of Jake who placed his hand on her belly and smiled at the camera.  Jake shared the following caption along with the following photo.

A true labor of love. One of the best parts of my job is working with dreamers, people who think big and manifest their passions. @chrissyteigen thank you for letting me into your Cravings HQ and making it a placewhere you and your team can be creative and chic all at the same time. @cravingsbychrissyteigen ❤️ lastly thank you to my amazing team who make shit happen like no other!!

Some fans were confused by the shot and didn’t understand why Chrissy was posing with Jake and not her husband, John. Chrissy and Jake posed in such a manner that some people who weren’t aware of who her husband was thought she was actually with Jake. This is when Katy Perry shared a hilarious comment that quickly went viral.

Katy Perry wrote the following in the comment section under Jake Arnold’s photo.

“Omg you’re the daddy!”
