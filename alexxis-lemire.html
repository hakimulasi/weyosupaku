Alexxis Lemire

Birth Name: Alexxis C. Lemire

Place of Birth: Londonderry, New Hampshire, U.S.

Date of Birth: May 30, 1996

Ethnicity:
*father – French-Canadian
*mother – Puerto Rican

Alexxis Lemire is an American actress.

She is the daughter of Hilda and James Lemire. She has said “My mom is 100 percent Puerto Rican,” and that she is “half Puerto Rican and half French.”

Alexxis’ paternal grandfather is named Robert J. “Bob” Lemire.

Alexxis’ paternal grandmother is Diane C. Mercier (the daughter of Leo Mercier and Jean Marcotte).
