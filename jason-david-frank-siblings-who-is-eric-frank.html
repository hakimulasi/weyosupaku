Jason David Frank Siblings: Who Is Eric Frank?

Jason David Frank had a brother named Eric Frank. He was an American actor who played David Trueheart, the older and long-lost brother of Tommy Oliver, on Power Rangers: Zeo.

He was the real-life older brother of actor, Jason David Frank.

Eric died on April 16, 2001at the age of 29 after an unspecific illness.

Eric worked behind the scenes with Dennis Bishop, who at the time, worked on the show as a gaffer. His brother was the one that landed him the job.

There isn’t much information about Eric’s personal life on the internet.
