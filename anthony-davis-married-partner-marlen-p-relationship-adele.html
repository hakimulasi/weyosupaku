Anthony Davis is married to his partner Marlen P in a ceremony attended by Adele!

  • Los Angeles Laker Anthony Davis is married to partner Marlen P
  • Their wedding was attended by his teammates, coach, and Adele
  • He and Marlen have been dating since 2016

Los Angeles Lakers Anthony Davis is married! The NBA star married his longtime partner Marlen P recently. Their wedding was attended by his teammates LeBron James and Russell Westbrook, University of Kentucky basketball coach John Calipari and Adele.

For the grand wedding, Anthony wore a traditional black tuxedo with a white button-down shirt. His beautiful wife wore a tight, low-cut white wedding gown. For the reception, Davis changed into a smart black dinner suit with a similarly colored black button-down and gray slacks.

Anthony went to the dance floor with his wife and sang Never Make a Promise while the pair danced. They dated for a long time before taking it to marriage. The couple started dating in 2016 but remained private about their affair. They made their first public appearance together at the Vanity Fair Oscars Party in 2020. Likewise, the couple has a daughter Nala who was born in 2017.
