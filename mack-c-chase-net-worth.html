Mack C. Chase Net Worth

What is Mack C. Chase's Net Worth?

Mack C. Chase is an American businessman who has a net worth of $910 million. Mack C. Chase founded Mack Energy Corporation and works as the company's president; he created his business after spending more than 50 years working in the oil and gas fields during his lifetime. Chase remains active with the daily operations of the company. Mack Energy Corporation operates in a handful of sectors, including farming, aviation, insurance, ranching, drilling, piping, and real estate.

Over his lifetime, Chase has founded several companies such as Texas Deerhorn Aviation, Avion, the Arrowhead Pipe and Supply Company, Carlsbad Insurance Company, and Marbob Energy Corporation. Chase, who graduated from Artesia High School in 1950 in New Mexico, has given back to his community many times over the years. Chase also placed the headquarters of his business in Artesia. He organized a scholarship program for current students and donated more than one million dollars towards their college education. The Artesia Chamber of Commerce recognized Chase as the Businessman of the Year in the early '80s. He also earned Boss of the Year honors from an organization in his hometown and Corporate Business of the Year accolades from the chamber of commerce as well.
