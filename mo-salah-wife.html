Mo Salah Wikipedia

Mo Salah Wikipedia: - Mohamed Salah Hamed Mahrous Ghaly was born on 15 June 1992 in Nagrig, Basyoun, Egypt. He is an Egyptian professional footballer. He plays for Premier League club Liverpool as a right winger or forward. He is the captain of Egypt national team. He is mainly known for his clinical finishing, dribbling and speed. He is one of the best players of his generation who began his senior career in 2010 from Al Mokawloon. He join Basel in 2012 and won two Swiss Super League titles. He signed for Liverpool in 2017 with the deal of £36.9 million. He has won two PFA Players' Player of the Year awards, three Premier League Golden Boots, the Premier League Player of the Season, the Premier League Playmaker of the Season. Salah represented Egypt at youth level at international level.
