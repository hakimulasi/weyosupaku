Robert Downey Jr shares first photo of daughter Avri

    Robert Downey Jr has finally given his fans a first look at his new daughter Avri. Five months after the youngster made her way into the world on 4 November 2014, her proud father shared a sweet snapshot of his baby girl on with David Letterman. "Congratulations! We have another baby, this is a little girl," the host told Robert."Yes, we have a daughter named Avri," replied the star. "I have three kids now, I have a 21-year-old named Indio, I have a three-year-old named Exton and now Avri who is now a little over five months."I thought I'd show the first picture on planet earth here with you kind sir," he added. "I would love [to show] it."

    The adorable picture of little Avri, which shows her sporting a sweet yellow woollen hat, was met with a loud 'aw' from the audience while Robert said "You've got to love a yellow bonnet. "Keen to find out how Robert's sons have been reacting to the new addition to the family, 68-year-old David said: "What's the dynamic with this one and the older one? No rivalry?""No, not yet," responded the New York-born star, laughingly adding: "I mean it's natural for Exton to want to bite her every once in a while and it can start off as a little pet and it turns into a nibble. "The chatshow host was keen to reassure the actor that children "have a way of figuring it all out.""They do," replied Robert. "I think it's just establishing dominance, but then she'll be running stuff at some point."
