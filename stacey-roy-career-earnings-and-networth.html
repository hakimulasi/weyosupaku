Stacey Roy career earnings and networth

Stacey Roy is a famous Canadian actress, TV host, and twitch streamer as well as an occasional model. She was born in Banff, Alberta, Canada to Teresa and Kurtis Roy. She has crowned Miss Teen Red Deer and Miss Teen Calgary in 2005.
