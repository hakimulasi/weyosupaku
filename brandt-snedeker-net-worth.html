What is Brandt Snedeker's Net Worth?

Brandt Snedeker is an American professional golfer who has a net worth of $20 million dollars. Brandt Newell Snedeker was born in Nashville, Tennessee in December 1980. His grandmother managed a golf course and introduced him to the game. Brandt attended Vanderbilt University. In 2003 before turning professional he won the U.S. Amateur Public Links. In 2004 he turned pro and played on the Nationwide Tour until 2006. In 2007 he joined the PGA Tour and was named PGA Tour Rookie of the Year. Snedeker has wins including six on the PGA Tour.

In 2008 he finished 3rd in the Masters Tournament. He also finished 3rd in the 2012 The Open Championship. In 2012 he won the FedEx Cup and moved into the top ten of the Official World Golf Ranking. Snedeker won the AT&T Pebble Beach National Pro-Am in 2013 and was then ranked #4 in the world. He has also won the 2007 Wyndham Championship, 2011 The Heritage, 2012 Farmers Insurance Open, 2012 Tour Championship, and 2013 RBC Canadian Open. He has appeared for the U.S. national team, winning the Presidents Cup in 2013. He lives in Nashville with his wife Mandy and his two children.
