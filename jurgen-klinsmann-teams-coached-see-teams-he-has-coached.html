International Career

After making his debut for West Germany in 1987, Klinsmann went on to earn 108 caps for the nation, moving him into fourth place behind Lothar Matthäus, Miroslav Klose, and Lukas Podolski in terms of caps.

Only Klose’s record of 71 goals for the national team, Gerd Müller’s 68 goals, and Podolski’s 49 goals are greater than Klinsmann’s 47 goals, which he shared with Rudi Völler for fourth place all-time.

Klinsmann placed sixth all-time with 11 goals at the FIFA World Cup.
