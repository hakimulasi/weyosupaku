Ao Ashi Chapter 350 Release Date, Spoiler, Raw Scan, Countdown &amp; Everything You Need to Know

Ao Ashi Chapter 350:- Ao Ashi is a popular manga series that follows the journey of Ashito Aoi, a talented soccer player from Ehime who dreams of becoming a professional. The manga is written by Yugo Kobayashi and illustrated by Naohiko Ueno and has been serialized in Weekly Big Comic Spirits since 2015. It has won several awards, including the Manga Taisho Award in 2017 and the Kodansha Manga Award in 2018.

The manga is currently in its 35th volume, and the latest chapter, 349, was released on November 1, 2023. In this chapter, we saw the intense match between Tokyo City Esperion and Yokohama Flugels, where Ashito and his teammates faced their rivals and former friends. The chapter ended with a cliffhanger, as Ashito scored a goal in the last minute, but the referee blew the whistle before the ball crossed the line.

So, what will happen in the next chapter, 350? When will it be released, and where can you read it? Here are all the details you need to know.
